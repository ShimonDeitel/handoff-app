Whose-turn rotation

Siblings take turns automatically. Everyone can see, at a glance, exactly whose turn it is right now — no more group-text guessing.

Quick visit logs

A short note and an optional photo after every visit or call. Light enough to actually keep up with, visible to the whole circle instantly.

The handoff note has to be read

The outgoing sibling leaves a note. The next sibling has to actually open it — a real read timestamp, not just "delivered" — before their turn is officially theirs.

AI weekly digest

One warm, specific sentence condensing the whole week's notes — written for whoever lives too far away to visit in person.
