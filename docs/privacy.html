Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Handoff is built for one purpose: helping siblings coordinate care for a parent. This policy explains, plainly, what data the app touches and where it goes.

Your family's data lives in your family's iCloud — not on our servers

Everything you enter in Handoff — the circle's setup, sibling names and photos, visit logs, and handoff notes — is stored using Apple's CloudKit, in a private iCloud database owned by whoever set up the care circle. That person shares access to their private data zone directly with the siblings they invite, using Apple's own CKShare sharing mechanism (the same technology behind Shared Albums and Family Sharing).

Handoff does not operate its own database and cannot read your family's circle data. We have no admin panel, no analytics dashboard, and no way to see your visit logs, photos, or handoff messages. Apple's iCloud terms govern that storage; see Apple's privacy policy for how iCloud itself handles data.

The AI weekly digest (Pro feature)

If you use the Pro weekly digest, the text of that week's visit logs is sent to a Cloudflare Worker we operate, which forwards it to a Workers AI text model to write one summary sentence. The request is stateless: nothing is written to a database, and the Worker does not retain a copy of what was sent after the response is returned. No account identifier, email, or persistent device ID travels with that request — just the visit-log text needed to write the sentence.

Photos

Photos you attach to a visit log or a sibling's profile are stored the same way as everything else — through your family's own CloudKit sharing, never on a separate server operated by us. The app asks for photo-library access only to let you pick a photo; it never scans or uploads your library beyond the specific photo you select.

What we don't do

  • No advertising, no ad networks, no third-party trackers.
  • No selling or sharing of your data with anyone.
  • No account system beyond your own Apple ID / iCloud sign-in.
  • No location tracking.

What stays on your device

Handoff keeps a local cache of your circle's data on your device (in the app's private storage area) so the app opens instantly and keeps working without a network connection. This local cache is not accessible to other apps and is deleted if you delete the app or use "Erase Local Data" in Settings.

Subscriptions

Handoff Pro is an auto-renewable subscription processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never see or store your payment details — Apple handles billing, and your subscription status is confirmed to the app only through Apple's StoreKit framework.

Children's privacy

Handoff is intended for adult siblings coordinating care for a parent and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes in a way that affects how your data is handled, we'll update this page and the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent through the App Store support link on Handoff's product page.
