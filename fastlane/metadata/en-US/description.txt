When adult siblings share the care of an aging parent, the coordination usually lives in a group chat, a shared document, or a paper calendar taped to the fridge. Nobody is ever completely sure whose turn it is, what happened at the last visit, or whether the next person actually got the message.

Handoff gives your family one small, calm place for all of it. The Circle tab shows every sibling as a node in a constellation around your parent, with a rotating turn schedule so it is always clear who is up next. After a visit or a call, log a short note with an optional photo in seconds. When your turn ends, you leave a handoff note for whoever is next, and Handoff tracks when they actually open and read it. Until they do, the whole circle sees that the baton has not been picked up yet. Everything syncs through iCloud using Apple's built-in sharing, so there is no account to create and your family's notes stay in your family's iCloud.

The Visits tab keeps a running history of every note. The Weekly Digest, part of Pro, condenses the week's visit notes into one warm sentence for the sibling who lives far away. Digest text is generated by AI and processed via our servers; only the week's visit note text is sent, and no account is needed.

Handoff is free for a circle of up to 2 family members with your 5 most recent visit logs. Handoff Pro is an auto-renewing monthly subscription at $7.99 per month, billed once per family circle rather than per person, and unlocks unlimited members, full visit history, and the AI Weekly Digest.

Care is easier when the baton never gets dropped.
